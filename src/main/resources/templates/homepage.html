About us

Welcome to Paradise, your haven of tranquility in the heart of Nepal's enchanting city. Nestled amidst the breathtaking Himalayan landscape, our hotel offers a perfect blend of comfort and hospitality. Immerse yourself in the warm Nepali culture as you indulge in our delectable local cuisine and explore the wonders of Nepal. Whether it's a leisurely vacation or an adventurous getaway, let Paradise be your home away from home. Come, experience the essence of Nepal with us.
